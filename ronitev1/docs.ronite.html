How to Play

A complete walkthrough from setting up your wallet to earning and selling ore rewards.

Step 1 — Wallet setup

Install the Ronin Wallet browser extension or mobile app. Connect it to Ronin Mainnet (chain ID 2020). Make sure you have a RON balance to buy RONITE and pay gas fees on every transaction.

Step 2 — Buy RONITE

In the "Buy RONITE" section, enter how much RON you want to spend. The preview shows how many RONITE you'll receive. Click Buy RONITE and confirm the transaction. Fixed rate: 1 RON = 10 RONITE.

Step 3 — Start mining

Pick an ore pool and enter your RONITE amount in the Stake field. On your first stake per pool, click Approve to grant the contract permission — this is only required once. Then click Stake and mining starts immediately.

Step 4 — Claim rewards

Ore rewards appear in real time on each pool card. Click Claim on a specific pool, or use Claim All in the banner at the top to collect all pools at once (one transaction per pool).

Step 5 — Sell ore

Open the Sell tab on any pool card. Enter how much ore you want to sell — the preview shows estimated RONITE received. Selling requires two steps: Approve the ore token, then Sell. A minimum applies per transaction based on the ore's exchange rate.

Important: Every action requires a wallet confirmation and a small amount of RON for gas. Make sure your RON balance doesn't run out mid-session.
You don't need to unstake to claim rewards. Ore keeps accumulating as long as RONITE is staked and the mining period is active.

Reward formula

How daily rewards are estimated

Your daily reward depends on your share of the total pool:

est/day = (yourStake / totalStake) × rewardRate × 86400

This value changes dynamically as other miners enter or leave. A larger share = more ore per day. The live reward counter in the UI updates every second.

FAQ

Answers to the most common questions about Ronite Miner.

Can I sell RONITE back for RON?
Not directly through this dapp. RONITE is used to mine ore, which can then be sold back to RONITE via the OreMarket. Secondary market trading on DEXes like Katana may be available depending on liquidity.
Is there any risk of losing my staked RONITE?
Staked RONITE remains locked in the contract and can be withdrawn at any time with no penalty. Main risks are smart contract bugs (contracts are under audit) and RON price volatility. Always consider DeFi risks before participating.
Why do staking and selling require two transactions?
This is the standard ERC-20 security pattern. The first transaction (Approve) grants the contract permission to spend your tokens. The second transaction (Stake or Sell) executes the action. Approval is only required once per token per contract.
Why aren't my rewards increasing?
Likely causes: (1) The mining period has ended — check "Period ends" on the pool card. (2) Your stake transaction hasn't been confirmed yet. (3) Your staked RONITE balance is 0. Refresh the page and verify your transaction on Ronin Explorer.
Can I stake in multiple pools at the same time?
Yes! You can stake RONITE across all four pools simultaneously. Each pool is fully independent — rewards and balances are tracked separately. A common strategy is to diversify based on risk appetite and desired ore type.
Is there a minimum stake amount?
No enforced minimum at the contract level. That said, your stake should justify the gas cost per transaction. Ronin gas fees are very low — typically under $0.01 — so even small stakes are economical.
How do I get RON to get started?
RON can be purchased on Katana DEX (Ronin's native exchange) or on centralized exchanges like Binance, then bridged to Ronin. You can also use the Ronin Bridge to move ETH or other assets from Ethereum to the Ronin network.
Is the source code open source?
Yes. All .sol files (RoniteToken, RewardToken, MiningStaking, OreMarket) are publicly available and verifiable on Ronin Explorer. The frontend is also open source in this repository.
